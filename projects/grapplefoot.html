Grapplefoot

Combine your grappling hook and a powerful kick to defeat enemies, destroy the “power gems” and escape the arena.

You play as “Grapplefoot”, an ancient strongman who was cryogenically preserved. Now unfrozen in the future, you have become the subject of a series of simulation experiments run by the SimuTECH corporation.

Watch your head!

EFLW CCN (Eco-Friendly Leg Workout: Color-Coded Nitro (Vol.1)) is a 3D platformer created during Global Game Jam 2020 in Prague. The theme we were given was "Repair" but as the game was developing we dropped it in favor of a word combination that we initially only planned to use as a name.

Your task as a player is to collect a key and find your way on top of the highest platform on the map. To help you reach the heights you are provided with a portable gun loaded with 3 types of ammo. Even though the pellets are dangerous, loaded with colored magma, your main task is to shoot the lit platforms. When a platform is hit it changes its color based on the type of ammo used and so do its features. The currently loaded pellet can be seen In the very top slot of the drum magazine.

List of colors&features:

Blue: Jump Boost

Green: Speed Boost

Yellow: Wall Run Boost

Red: Rotate

Gameplay

Art

I was responsible for all things visual. From menu to environments everything was created on-site. To save up some time I decided to utilize Unity's Shadergraph plugin and create a few shaders that I could use on the models. This way i cut the work time on each prop by a hefty amount because I could skip the the unwrapping and texturing.

For the colors I used simple gradient shader based on normal position. For the Ammo pellets, I used emissive material combined with some noise and fresnel.

Conclusion

This was my second Global Game Jam and 4th jam at that site and it was as terrific as always. The game turned out great and it was really fun working and chatting with all the people there. I'm really looking forward to the next one.
