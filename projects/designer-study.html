Old Soviet Camera

This is a small project i decided to take up to improve at modeling and texturing game ready assets based on an already existing object. Everything was modeled in 3DS Max, textured in Substance Painter and rendered in Marmoset Toolbag. The cells texture for the top camera cover was made in Substance Designer.
