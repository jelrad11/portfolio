Beehave

Collect pollen, learn new skills and fend off a hungry bear with your baby bee army. But it comes at a cost. Are you cruel enough to sacrifice baby bees?

Site: Prague

  • Project folder

BBBees

BeeHave is all about collecting and upgrading. You as a player start with no pollen and only one flower to your disposal but as you progress through the game, collecting pollen from flowers, you'll be able to purchase not only more flowers, faster flying speed, dash, but also bear repellant, and most importantly, baby bees (or bee defenders). Those bees fly around the hive and protect it from the bear lurking around. With each attack, the bear gets stronger and fiercer, taking more bees to defend the hive. The health and attack of the bear is indicated above its head, with green cubes. Hive's health can be seen above the hive. The game ends when there are no bees to defend the hive and no green cubes of the hive are left.

Responsibilities

I was responsible for everything visual, 3D models and shaders, except UI. We initially planned to go with minimalistic to none UI but we up ended being short on time so we utilized the menus we used for testing.

Tadeas and Riccardo did the programming part. I'm looking forward to working with them again.
