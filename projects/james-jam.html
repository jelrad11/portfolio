James' Jam

Defend yourself from an army of hungry strawberries, make jam and cook cakes!

Summary

Event: Ryan's Lil Jam V1 (2019)

Site: Online

Length: 48 Hours

Theme: Produce

Team: Radim Jelínek, Sophia Utami, Skao

Responsible for: Programming, Source control and Art implementation (solo programmer)

Game engine: Unity

Platform: PC

  • Project folder

hunger

James' Jam is a game jam side scroller hack&slash game. You as a player are tasked with collecting oversized strawberries and making them into jam that goes on the cakes. But watch out, the strawberries will try to defend themselves! Fret not though, you were equipped with a strawberry skewer to speed up the process and keep you from dangers.

Gameplay

C# Magic

There was a critical lack of programmers. The community that took part in the jam was mostly consisting of artists and there wasn't that many people that could actually put their art into an engine and make it into a game. I had been learning programming for the "back then" past year or so and this proved to be a great opportunity to test out my skills. I also got to lead the team and handle version control.

Contributions

I was responsible for the programming side of the project, taking care of all the mechanics, implementations, etc. while also assuming the position of a lead. The animations and art were mostly made by Skao, Sophia also took part in the creation of art assets but unfortunately couldn't work on the game as much.
