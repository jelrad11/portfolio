Christmas Scene

This is an older scene I made to test out the "back then" newly released Rendering Engine Arnold (MAXtoA) for 3DS Max. It took roughly 16 hours to render on I5 6600 even though all the lights were optimized as much as possible. The biggest hurdle for the engine was probably the wreath combined with DOF. For the background I used HDRI map and the scene is also lit by the same map. Some lights were added on top to highlight details.
