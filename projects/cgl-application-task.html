My awesome Game

CGL application task 2020

[Game Name] is a 3D sidescroller/puzzle game targeted for mobile devices, offering an insight into a potential dystopian future where the world is engulfed in flames and the last of creatures are fighting for their lives on daily basis. This game is targeted on the teen to young adult audience.

This game takes place on a planet not too different from ours. You, as one of the remaining survivors, are forced out of your house. On the journey, you'll stumble on other creatures fighting for their lives and only together you can find a better place. [Game Name] is about survival.

Gestures

There are several gestures for each event. For example, if a dark cloud appears right above the egg the player has to form a roof-like shape/gesture to protect it from the rain. As the rain falls from the cloud it bounces of the hands and so the egg doesn't get wet.

In another scenario, winter comes and all the leaves fall from the tree and bushes. The player needs to move his hand up and down around the egg to warm it up or it freezes.

The player can see the egg's health in the top left-hand corner. If the egg gets damaged, it cracks a bit. If the egg cracks 3 times it's game over.

In this game, the player is trying to defend and protect the egg from dangers with his own hands. For the hands tracking we used a piece of hardware called Motion Leap, which is an Infra-red optical tracker that works rather well with Unity Engine. The game was initially thought of as a 2 person CO-OP game but it proved to be rather tricky to form gestures so we decided to stick with one player only.
